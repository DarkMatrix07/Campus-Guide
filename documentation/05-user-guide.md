# User Guide

## Students

### Signing Up
1. Go to `/register`
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Select **Student** as your role
4. You'll be taken to the student dashboard

### Browsing Businesses
- The student dashboard shows all approved businesses
- Use the **search bar** to filter by name or location
- Use the **category filter** to narrow by type (Food, Stationery, PG, etc.)
- Use the **rating filter** to show only businesses above a minimum star rating
- Click **View details** on any card to open the full business page

### Writing a Review
1. Open a business detail page
2. Select a star rating (1–5)
3. Write a short review in the text area
4. Click **Submit review**
5. You can only review each business once

### Reacting to Reviews
- On any review, click the **thumbs up** or **thumbs down** button
- Clicking the same button again removes your reaction
- Clicking the opposite button switches your reaction

### Top Rated Page
- Click **Top rated** in the student dashboard header
- Businesses are grouped by category and ranked by average student rating

### Editing Your Profile
- Click the **pencil icon** in the top navigation bar
- Update your name or email and click **Save changes**
- To change your password, type a new password — fields for confirm and current password will appear

## Business Owners

### Registering a Business
1. Sign up with the **Owner** role (or ask an admin to change your role)
2. On the owner dashboard, fill in the registration form:
   - Business name
   - Directory category (the broad category shown in the student directory)
   - Business type (specific description, e.g. "South Indian Restaurant")
   - Location
   - Description
   - Contact number
   - Storefront image (JPG, PNG, WebP or GIF, max 5MB)
3. Submit — your listing enters **Pending** status
4. An admin will review and approve or reject it

### Listing Statuses
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Submitted, waiting for admin review |
| Approved | Live in the student directory |
| Rejected | Not approved — contact admin for details |

### Editing Your Listing
1. On the owner dashboard, click **Edit listing**
2. Update any fields — upload a new image if needed (leave blank to keep the current one)
3. Click **Save changes**

### Deleting Your Listing
1. On the owner dashboard, click **Delete listing**
2. A confirmation appears — click **Confirm delete** to permanently remove it
3. The listing and its image are deleted immediately

### Viewing Reviews
- Scroll down on the owner dashboard to see all student reviews
- Star rating and average are displayed at the top of the reviews section

## Admins

### Approving Businesses
1. Go to the **Approvals** tab
2. Review the business details, image, owner info, and contact
3. Click **Approve** to make it live in the directory, or **Reject** to decline it

### Managing Categories
1. Go to the **Categories** tab
2. Type a new category name and click **Add**
3. Click the trash icon next to a category to delete it
4. Categories appear in the owner registration form and the student directory filters

### Managing Businesses
1. Go to the **Businesses** tab
2. Use the search bar to find a specific business
3. Click **Hide** to remove a business from the public directory without deleting it
4. Click the trash icon → **Confirm delete** to permanently remove it

### Managing Users
1. Go to the **Users** tab
2. Use the search bar to find a user by name or email
3. Change a user's role using the dropdown — takes effect immediately
4. Click the trash icon → **Confirm** to delete an account
5. You cannot modify your own account from this tab

### Managing Reviews
1. Go to the **Reviews** tab
2. Use the search bar to find reviews by business name or review text
3. Click **Hide** to hide a review from students without deleting it
4. Click the trash icon → **Confirm** to permanently delete it

### Editing Your Profile
- Same as students and owners — click the pencil icon in the header
